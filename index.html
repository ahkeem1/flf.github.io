Information

Like many of you, we have had to change the way that we provide services during this Pandemic. Also,
like many of you, we continue to seek to grow our connections to community partners and constituents
in our new “normal”. This is why it’s more important than ever for all of us to work closer together and
expand our community outreach capacity to enrich the lives of those we serve.
To this end, Family Legacy would like to extend our remote services to you and your constituents. To
reciprocate, we would like to refer our clients for your services, if needed. Therefore, please let us know
your mission and what services your organizations are providing to the community. Please complete the
attached Partner Organization Information form to describe your programs/services and contact
information.
As a reminder, all services offered by Family Legacy are FREE to you and your constituents!
